Interests

My life has been an adventure driven by a passion for staying active and exploring the world. From the rush of carving through fresh powder on a snowboard to riding the waves beneath the warm sun, I thrive on the exhilaration of physical pursuits. Traveling has been my greatest teacher, allowing me to embrace diverse cultures, languages, and landscapes.

One of the highlights of my journey was spending two unforgettable years living overseas in Canada, indulging in the thrill of a snow season. The crisp mountain air and the camaraderie with fellow snow enthusiasts left me with cherished memories that will last a lifetime.

Equally memorable was my four-month stint working at a surf camp in Colares, Portugal. The blend of teaching eager surfers, sharing beachfront bonfires, and immersing myself in the laid-back Portuguese lifestyle created an experience that truly enriched my life.

Music has always been the soundtrack to my adventures, bringing people together in joyful harmony. Problem-solving, another of my passions, challenges me to continually seek innovative solutions, whether it's conquering a challenging wave or unraveling a complex coding dilemma. Socializing, for me, is about connecting with others, forging bonds over shared experiences and making lifelong friends.

My life revolves around these passions, and they have shaped me into the adventurous and adaptable individual I am today.
